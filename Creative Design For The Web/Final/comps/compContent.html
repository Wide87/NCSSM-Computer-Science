Competitons

Competition at UNC-Asheville

Our first competition of the year, located at UNC Asheville. It usually takes place in the second week of March as a Week 3 Competition.

Event Code: NCASH

Competition in Mecklenberg County

Our second competition of the year, located at Palisades High School in Mecklenberg County. It usually takes place in the third week of March as a Week 4 Competition.

Event Code: NCMEC

State and Worlds

North Carolina's State Competition usually takes place at ECU in Pitt County, with about 40 teams coming from all over the state. Worlds always takes place in Houston, Texas. We've been to State twice, and have never been to Worlds yet.
